Available Helplines

Childline South Africa

Childline provides 24/7, free and confidential support over phone and online chat. We exist to help youth in South Africa who need assistance.

Call: 0800 055 555

Website: childline.org.za

Stop Gender Violence Helpline

Stop Gender Violence Helpline offers 24/7, free and confidential support and information over the phone. We support everyone in need.

Call: 0800 150 150

WhatsApp: 079 700 2000

Website: saferspaces.org.za

South African Federation For Mental Health Helpline

The South African Federation For Mental Health Helpline provides free, compassionate, and confidential support by phone. We help those in need of mental health support.

Call: 0861 322 322

Website: mentalhealthsa.org.za
